Dungeon Runner

  • Conquer Dungeon from another player
  • A running video game
  • Mobile Application

Overview

In Dungeon Runner, the player controls the character. The character chosen will embark on an adventure to conquer a custom Dungeon from another player. They will not realize that the Dungeon is inhabited by enemies, traps and secret passages . As the game is a running game, there is no time to stop; the player plays until the character collides into a large obstacle, falls to their death or makes it out of the dungeon .

While the character is running, the player can swipe left or right to move the character to either side of the screen to collect and/or avoid obstacles. There are three types of obstacles to be found while the character is running: treasure, traps, enemies. When the player needs to turn left or right, the touchscreen can be swiped in the corresponding direction. If the player wishes to jump over an object, the screen can be swiped upwards; if the player wishes to slide under an object, the screen can be swiped downwards.
