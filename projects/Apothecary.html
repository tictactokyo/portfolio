Breath of Icarus - Apothecary

  • Strategy video game
  • Test your time management
  • Can later become a mobile game

Overview

Gameplay involves taking orders and guiding around the room to serve orders. Gameplay centers around catering to orders to gather as much money as possible. The character can be moved around the room to prepare the order, bringing the order to the lift and complete tasks. As orders arrive where they sit down the player must guide the character to the lift to take the orders, then move them onto a table. After the order is prepared, the player must deliver the order. When the orders are finished eating they must be taken to the service hatch, at which point the orders leave a tip and their dishes on the table. The dishes must be cleared before the next set of orders can use the lift. Each successful action earns the player points.

Orders have a series of hearts over their heads that indicate their mood. The longer the customer is forced to wait, the more hearts is lost. Each type of customer has different degrees of patience and tipping habits. A number of actions can be performed, such as talking to order or serving them samples, to revive these hearts. When order lose all their hearts, they charge the player, costing the player points. The goal of a level is to earn a certain number of points. If enough money is earned after each level, the player progresses to the next. As the game progresses, the player can update the dilapidated basement.
