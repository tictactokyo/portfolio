Necropolis

  • Real-time strategy game
  • Cooperative first-person shooter

Overview

The game's story revolves around the two sides of a conflict, and each sides' attempts to survive and adapt over the others. The player assumes two roles through the course of the two campaigns: a SWAT commander tasked with defending the survivors to ensure the survival of their species, and a Zombie necromancer pushing forward the species' doctrine of assimilation. Set during the aftermath of a zombie outbreak, the game pits its protagonists—dubbed the "Survivors"—against hordes of zombies. The expansion's story continues only days after the conclusion of the original game. It introduces new features and improvements. A total of x new units with different functions and abilities are included, the artificial intelligence behavior was modified, new graphical tilesets for terrain were added and the game's level editor received improved scripting tools to facilitate cut scenes with the in-game engine.
