Circuit

  • Multiplayer online battle arena video game
  • Follows a freemium model
  • Supported by microtransactions

Overview

In Circuit, players assume the role of a "denizen" that controls a "racer" with unique abilities and battle against a team of other players and computer-controlled racers. The goal is usually to destroy the opposing team's "racers" and finish with the shortest time possible, although other distinct game modes exist as well. Each Circuit match is discrete, with all racers starting off fairly strong but decreasing in strength by losing fuel and items over the course of the match. The racer and setting blend a variety of elements, including cyberpunk, AR sim, and battle racing troupes.
